About the PRASHAD Scheme

The PRASHAD scheme focuses on the integrated development of identified pilgrimage and heritage destinations, providing a world-class tourism experience.

Objectives

  • Enhance tourism infrastructure and connectivity.
  • Promote local culture and heritage.
  • Boost employment opportunities in pilgrimage towns.
  • Provide a spiritual and cultural experience for visitors.
