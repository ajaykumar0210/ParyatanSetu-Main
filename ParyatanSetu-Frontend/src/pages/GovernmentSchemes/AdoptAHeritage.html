Adopt a Heritage Scheme

Preserving India's iconic monuments through public-private partnerships.

About the Scheme

The **Adopt a Heritage: Apni Dharohar, Apni Pehchaan** scheme is a collaborative initiative by the Ministry of Tourism, Archaeological Survey of India, and state governments. It aims to preserve and enhance India's heritage sites through public and private sector participation.

Objectives

  • Ensure the proper upkeep of heritage sites.
  • Enhance the tourism experience at monuments.
  • Engage public and private organizations in conservation.
  • Promote heritage awareness among citizens.

Roles of Stakeholders

  • Monument Mitras: Adopt and maintain heritage sites.
  • Government: Provide permissions and monitor progress.
  • Public: Participate in heritage conservation efforts.
