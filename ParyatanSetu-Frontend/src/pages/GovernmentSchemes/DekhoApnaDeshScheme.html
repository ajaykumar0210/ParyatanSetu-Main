About Dekho Apna Desh

The **Dekho Apna Desh Scheme** is an initiative by the Government of India to encourage citizens to explore the rich culture, heritage, and natural beauty of their country, promoting sustainable domestic tourism.

Key Objectives

  • Boost domestic tourism and create awareness about local destinations.
  • Encourage responsible and sustainable tourism practices.
  • Highlight India's lesser-known tourist spots.
  • Foster a sense of pride and connection among citizens for their country.

Activities Under the Scheme

  • Organizing webinars and virtual tours of popular destinations.
  • Incentives for domestic travel enthusiasts.
  • Special campaigns to promote hidden gems of India.
  • Collaborations with state tourism boards.
