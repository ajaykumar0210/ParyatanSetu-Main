Swadesh Darshan Scheme

Promoting theme-based tourism circuits in India.

Overview

The Swadesh Darshan Scheme focuses on integrated development of theme-based tourist circuits across the country, contributing to sustainable and responsible tourism.

Objectives

  • Promote cultural and heritage value of the country.
  • Enhance connectivity and infrastructure for tourism.
  • Boost local economies through tourism.
  • Provide employment opportunities in tourism-related sectors.
